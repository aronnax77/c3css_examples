w3Css Cards Demo

C3CSS cards are intended to provide a paper like appearance on the page with a shaddow to make them look separate from the surface. c3.css provides three card classes 'w3-card', 'w3-card-2', and w3-card-4. These have shaddow borders of 1px, 2px and 4px respectively. The use of the cards can be seen on this page.
